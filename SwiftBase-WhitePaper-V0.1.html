Holder Reward:
S
B
As soon as you have at least a certain number of
SwiftBase Tokens you are an official SwiftBase
holder. From each transaction, 3% fees are split
evenly among all Swiftbase holders. This gives you
another opportunity to build passive income. But
beware, as soon as the user sells his SWIFTs, he no
longer receives a holder reward.
——————————————————————————————————————————————
Staking:
S
B
You can lock a certain number of SwiftBase tokens for a
month to get 1.5% return after the month. Locking
cryptocurrencies is called staking. Staking contributes
to the security and stability of the cryptocurrency.
——————————————————————————————————————————————
How to buy SwiftBase:
You can easily buy and sell SwiftBase with Matic on
SushiSwap.
At the very beginning, 20% of the total amount (1 million)
is offered for sale. As more SwiftBase is needed, this
number will increase to 80% (4 million). 20% (1 million) will
remain on our developer wallet to provide network
security and to conduct transactions such as staking.
